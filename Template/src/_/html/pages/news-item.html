Black and White Dress

05/08/2015

In the wardrobe of any woman should be clothes for work and formal occasions. Meetings, meetings and negotiations, corporate parties or presenting company at social events and other event'ah - all that is needed for a strict dress code. It, by the way, should not only be a serious and sustained, but also stylish and fashionable.

And today our focus is one of the most popular and strict formal codes - Black Tie: now its observance is a prerequisite to attend the various events. Therefore, in this review we take a closer look at a way how to be a black and white dress code in 2012. Also, we will touch upon the details of clothes that will make sustained look interesting and original. And be sure to see whether it can be considered to combine with other direction, for example, with the style of cocktail.
